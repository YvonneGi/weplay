About us

Sports is the best way to make yourself fit in all respects,It plays a vital role in our life as it keeps us healthy, wealthy and of course active.
it is also necessary to put more emphasis on Sports ,Despite the well known benefit of sport in our life ,a huge amount of people in rwanda don't exercise enough because,

  • not knowing where to exercise from
  • culture and financial stereotype
  • shortage of fitness activities
  • lack of appropriate knowledge regarding fitness activities
In terms of social interaction we decide to create a website that can help people to know and join the fitness activities nearby them.
